Chennai Mathematical Institute Mathematics Fields Medal Lecture Series -- January - April 2023

The Fields Medal Lecture Series will include a set of 4 talks by experts, regarding the work and impact of the recent Fields medallists. These talks are scheduled on Fridays from 2:00 pm to 3:00 pm, and are specifically aimed at undergraduate and graduate students. In particular, they will be accessible to a wide audience.

The schedule for the talks is listed below. Further details will be posted here soon!
