Chennai Mathematical Institute Mathematics Colloquium -- January - April 2023

There will be two types of colloquia this semester - One is a series of talks on works of Fields medalists announced in 2022. We will have four talks given by experts explaining the work and impact of the Fields medallists. These will happen on four Fridays during the semester. The timing will be Friday 2:00 - 3:00 pm. These are specifically aimed at undergraduate and graduate students and a wide audience. Schedule is listed below. We will also run a series of talks that will have expert researchers explaining their work. These will also feature colloquium talks aimed at undergraduate and graduate students and non-experts in general. The timing will be Wednesdays 3:30 - 4:30 pm. (But note that there is change of timings for some talks at the beginning!)
